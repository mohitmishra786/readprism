account you agree to them.

## The Service

ReadPrism aggregates content from sources and creators you choose and ranks it by
personal relevance. Ranking quality depends on behavioural signal that
accumulates over time; early digests are necessarily less personalized.

processing in your jurisdiction (generally 16 in the EU, 13 in the US).

are permitted to forward that content** and to have it processed on the Service.
Forwarded newsletters are treated as private to your account and are not shown to
other users. We may block or discard forwarded content that appears abusive.

original source. Summaries are machine-generated and may be inaccurate; the
original publisher owns their content. We honour robots.txt and identify our
fetcher honestly by default. If you are a content owner and want your content
excluded, contact the operator.

The Service performs automated fetching of public web content. Operators are
responsible for using it in compliance with applicable law and the terms of the
sites they fetch. The default posture identifies the fetcher as a bot and backs
off when blocked.
